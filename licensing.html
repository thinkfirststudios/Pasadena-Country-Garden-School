Licensing & compliance

Our license, our records, your right to see them.

California law gives families the right to this information. We'd rather you didn't have to ask.

Transparency statement

Pasadena Country Garden School is a licensed childcare facility operating under the California Department of Social Services, Community Care Licensing Division. We comply with Title 22, Division 12 of the California Code of Regulations, and with Aryanna's Law, which requires licensed childcare providers to make their licensing records available to families.

You have the right to see our licensing file — inspection reports, any notices, and any actions taken. You do not need a reason, and you do not need our permission. Everything we are required to publish is on this page, and the state's own copy is available to you directly at the contacts below.
