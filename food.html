Food

Food is the heart of our philosophy.

Nourishing Traditions is our approach. Organic meals, made from real ingredients, served free to every child.

[IMAGE: An organic lunch on the table]

Nourishing Traditions

Real food, made here

Most childcare treats food as logistics. We treat it as teaching. Children who help grow a carrot, wash it, and eat it at a table with their friends learn something no lesson delivers.

Our approach follows Nourishing Traditions: whole, organic ingredients, traditional preparation, nothing from a packet pretending to be food.

Meals provided

Three meals, no charge

Meals are provided free to every enrolled child. There is no lunch box to pack and no line item on your bill.

Allergies and dietary needs

Tell us everything at enrollment — allergies, intolerances, and medical needs. In a small kitchen cooking from scratch, accommodating a child is a conversation, not a special order.

[IMAGE: Children harvesting from the garden]

Garden to table

The shortest supply chain there is

The garden isn't decoration. Children plant it, water it, watch it take its time, and then eat it. That's where patience comes from, and where a child learns that food is something you tend rather than something that appears.
