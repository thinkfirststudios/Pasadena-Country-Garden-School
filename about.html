Alexandra Pike is a modern marvel. She homeschooled her two children from day one through high-school graduation. She is the gem you need — and want — to trust with your child.

Alexandra Pike is the owner and director of Pasadena Country Garden School.

Our philosophy

We take a holistic approach that emphasizes creativity and child-centered learning — integrating cognitive, emotional, and physical development based on the threefold nature of the human being: body, soul, and spirit.

Body

Physical development, in a gorgeous indoor/outdoor, nature-based environment with outdoor areas.

Soul

Emotional development, through creativity and child-centered learning.

Spirit

Cognitive development, where children are protected from the noise and stress of everyday life.
