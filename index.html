Welcome

We are much more than a daycare. Pasadena Country Garden School is a gorgeous indoor/outdoor, nature-based environment where children are protected from the noise and stress of everyday life.

A holistic approach to a whole child

We emphasize creativity and child-centered learning — integrating cognitive, emotional, and physical development based on the threefold nature of the human being: body, soul, and spirit.

Call and make an appointment to visit and meet Alexandra to really understand how special she and her devoted team are. We can't wait to meet you!

Meet Alexandra and the team

Our approach

Art, gardening, and organic food

Three simple things, done every day, that teach children how to make, how to tend, and how to share.

Waldorf-inspired art

Watercolor, beeswax, wool, and song. Children make things with their hands every day — the work of childhood, and the beginning of confidence.

Gardening & nature

Seeds go in, hands get dirty, and something grows. Our outdoor areas are the classroom — patience, care, and the seasons, learned first-hand.

Organic food

Real meals, made from real ingredients, served free to every child. Food is the heart of our philosophy — not an afterthought.

Alexandra Pike

Alexandra Pike is a modern marvel. She homeschooled her two children from day one through high-school graduation. She is the gem you need — and want — to trust with your child.
